Sri Aurobindo College — BCA Department

SAC

Sri Aurobindo College

Bachelor of Computer Applications

Sri Aurobindo College, affiliated with the University of Delhi and established under the aegis of the Sri Aurobindo Educational Trust, stands as one of Delhi's premier institutions of higher learning. Nestled in the intellectual heart of Malviya Nagar, the college upholds a long-standing tradition of academic excellence, holistic development, and a vibrant campus culture that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and leadership.

The Department of Computer Applications offers a rigorous three-year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) programme designed to equip students with a strong foundation in both the theoretical and practical dimensions of computing. The curriculum spans core areas including software development, object-oriented programming, data structures & algorithms, database management systems, web technologies, computer networking, and operating systems — all aligned with the rapidly evolving demands of the global IT industry.

Beyond the classroom, the department actively fosters a culture of innovation through hackathons, coding competitions, industry guest lectures, and collaborative project-based learning. Students graduate ready to pursue careers in software engineering, data analytics, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and entrepreneurship, or to further their studies in top-tier postgraduate programmes across India and abroad.

💻 Software Development 🗄️ Database Management 🌐 Web Technologies 🔗 Computer Networking 📐 Data Structures 🖥️ Operating Systems ☁️ Cloud Computing 🔐 Cybersecurity
